Travel Guidelines & Safety

Railway Safety Measures

  • Regular cleaning and sanitization of coaches
  • Security checks at station entry points
  • 24x7 helpline available for emergencies
  • Medical facilities available at major stations
  • Fire safety equipment in all coaches

Travel Tips

  • Arrive at the station at least 30 minutes before departure
  • Keep your ID proof ready for verification
  • Check PNR status before starting your journey
  • Label your luggage with your name and contact details
  • Be aware of your belongings at all times

Cancellation & Refund Policy
Cancellation Rules
Cancellation Timing Refund Percentage Cancellation Fee
More than 48 hours before departure 100% minus cancellation fee ₹ 240 for AC classes, ₹ 120 for Sleeper
Between 48 and 12 hours before departure 75% minus cancellation fee ₹ 240 for AC classes, ₹ 120 for Sleeper
Between 12 and 6 hours before departure 50% minus cancellation fee ₹ 240 for AC classes, ₹ 120 for Sleeper
Less than 6 hours before departure No refund Entire amount
Special Conditions
  • Tatkal tickets: Non-refundable except when cancelled by railways.
  • VIKALP tickets: Refund as per normal cancellation rules.
  • Train cancellation: Full refund without cancellation charges.
  • Partial cancellations allowed for group bookings with applicable charges.
Refund Process

Refunds are processed automatically to the original payment method used for booking. It can take 5-7 working days for the refund to reflect in your account. For any refund-related queries, please contact our customer support.
